Jessica from Reverse 1999

Introduction

Jessica is a 6✦ rarity character from the Plant afflatus who deals Reality damage and belongs to the Damage class. Jessica is a powerful Carry focused on dealing increased damage against enemies afflicted with Poison.

Skills

  • Gaze From the Forest: Deal massive damage and inflicts Poison on all enemies for 2 rounds


  • White Blankie: 1-target attack, if the target suffers Poison, deals more DMG


  • Good Friends: Mass attack, if the target is inflicted with Negative Status, deal more damage


Inheritance

  • Insight I: the damage dealt increases by 20% when attacking an enemy afflicted with Poison.
  • Insight II: DMG Dealt +8%
  • After attacking enemies not afflicted with Poison, inflicts Poison for 2 rounds.
