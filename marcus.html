Marcus from Reverse 1999

Introduction

Marcus is a 6✦ rarity character from the Plant afflatus who deals Mental damage and belongs to the Damage class.

Skills

  • Reading Class: 1-target attack. Deals 200% Mental DMG.


  • From the Lantern: Mass attack. Deals 130% Mental DMG to 2 enemies. Deals Mental DMG +30% to the target in the [Annotate] status.


  • Next Chapter: Mass attack. Deals 250% Mental DMG to all enemies and Mental DMG +200% to the target with [Annotate] status. Gain 1 stack of [Preparation - Marcus], and grant Eureka +2 to self.


Inheritance

  • Insight I: the damage dealt increases by 20% when attacking an enemy afflicted with Poison.
  • Insight II: DMG Dealt +8%
  • After attacking enemies not afflicted with Poison, inflicts Poison for 2 rounds.
