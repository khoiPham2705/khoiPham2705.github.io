Regulus from Reverse 1999

Introduction

Regulus is a 6✦ rarity character from the Star afflatus who deals Mental damage and belongs to the Sub DPS class.

Skills

  • Treat for the Ears: 1-target attack. Deals 200% Mental DMG.


  • Challenge for the Eyes: Mass attack. Deals 150% Mental DMG to 2 enemies.


  • Sleepless Rave: Mass attack. Deals 300% Mental DMG to all enemies and inflicts [Riot and Roll] status on all allies for 1 round.


Inheritance

  • Insight I: If the caster has not acted in a round, enters [Restless Heart] status at the end of the round.
  • Insight II: DMG Dealt +8%
  • When [Restless Heart] status is dispelled, Moxie +1.
